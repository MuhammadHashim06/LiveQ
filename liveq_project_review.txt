# LiveQ - Project Features & Functionalities Review

Based on the analysis of the project structure and codebase, here is a comprehensive breakdown of the features, modules, and functionalities present in the LiveQ application.

## ⚙️ Tech Stack & Architecture
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **Database**: MongoDB (via Mongoose)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Authentication**: Custom JWT-based Auth (bcrypt, jose, jsonwebtoken)
- **Real-time**: Socket.IO (for live queue updates and notifications)
- **Maps integration**: React Google Maps API
- **Charts & Visuals**: Recharts
- **Storage**: Cloudinary (for image/file uploads)
- **Emails**: Nodemailer

---

## 👥 User Roles
The platform operates with three primary user roles:
1. **Admin**: Platform managers overseeing users and businesses.
2. **Business**: Service providers who manage queues and appointments.
3. **Customer**: End-users who find businesses, book appointments, and join queues.

---

## 🔐 1. Authentication & Account Management
**Endpoints:** `src/app/api/auth/*` & `src/app/api/user/*`
- **Sign Up / Registration**
- **Login / Logout**
- **OTP Verification**: Email verification via OTP.
- **Resend Verification**: Re-trigger verification emails.
- **Password Management**: Forgot Password, Reset Password, and Change Password.
- **Profile Management**: Fetch and update personal user profile information (`/api/user/me`).

---

## 🏢 2. Business Features (Service Providers)
**Dashboard:** `src/app/dashboard/business/*`
**Endpoints:** `src/app/api/business/*`

- **Onboarding Flow**: Dedicated setup wizard for newly registered businesses.
- **Business Profile Settings**: Manage business details, description, and contact info.
- **Service Management**: Create, read, update, and delete specific services offered by the business.
- **Availability Management**: Set specific open/close hours and working days.
- **Location Setup**: Google Maps integration to set exact coordinates and address.
- **Appointments Management**: 
  - View upcoming and past customer bookings.
  - Customer Check-in functionality to transition an appointment into an active queue status.
- **Queue Management**:
  - View current active queue.
  - Drag-and-drop capability to reorder the queue.
- **Reviews & Feedback**: Monitor and read reviews submitted by customers.
- **Analytics & Dashboard Overview**: Graphical representation (Recharts) of business performance, queue stats, and daily bookings.

---

## 🙋 3. Customer Features (End Users)
**Dashboard:** `src/app/dashboard/customer/*`
**Endpoints:** `src/app/api/appointments/*` & `src/app/api/queue/customer/*`

- **Find Businesses**: Directory to search and locate registered active businesses using maps.
- **Business Details Page**: View a specific business's profile, services, and location.
- **Booking System**: 
  - Select services and book appointments with businesses.
  - View personal upcoming and past appointment history.
- **Queue Tracking**: Participate in a live queue and monitor real-time position status.
- **Early Arrival feature**: Functionality for customers to indicate they have arrived at the location early.
- **Entertainment Module**: In-app entertainment section to keep customers engaged while waiting in the queue.
- **Reviews**: Ability to leave reviews for businesses after service completion.

---

## 👑 4. Admin Features (Platform Management)
**Dashboard:** `src/app/dashboard/admin/*`
**Endpoints:** `src/app/api/admin/*`

- **Platform AnalyticsOverview**: High-level platform statistics (total users, active businesses, platform usage).
- **Business Management**:
  - View a list of all businesses.
  - View specific business details.
  - Verify and approve businesses (`/api/admin/businesses/[id]/verify`).
- **User Management**: 
  - View and manage all registered customer accounts.
- **Reports & Exports**: Functionality to export user and business data into downloadable formats.

---

## 🔔 5. Core System Entities & Modules
**Database Models:** `src/models/*`

- **User**: Base model for authentication, identifying admins, customers, or business owners.
- **Business**: Specific schema handling location data, operating hours, and business metadata.
- **Appointment**: Links a Customer to a Business for a specific scheduled time and service.
- **Queue**: Manages the current list of waiting customers, their positions, and real-time status.
- **Review**: Relationship between a Customer and Business containing ratings and textual feedback.
- **Notification**: System for sending out email and in-app alerts (reminders, queue updates, verification).
- **Uploads**: Dedicated API route to handle Cloudinary image/file uploads (used for profiles and business galleries).
